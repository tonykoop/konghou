- Use `assumption`, `TBD`, or `requires measurement` for unverified dimensions.
- Do not caption the DXF/OpenSCAD scaffold as build-ready geometry.
- Do not use generated concept images as evidence of string spacing, bridge
  location, bracing, soundboard crown, joinery, or load path.
- Keep any public image set behind the same gates as `validation.csv` and
  `frame-string-scaling.csv`.
